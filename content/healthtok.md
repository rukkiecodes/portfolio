## The idea

Getting to see a doctor shouldn't mean a half-day lost to traffic, a crowded waiting room, and a queue that moves slower than the illness. For millions of people — especially across Nigeria and similar markets — quality care is gated less by a shortage of good doctors than by the friction of *reaching* them.

HealthTok removes that friction. It puts a directory of verified doctors in your pocket, lets you talk to one in minutes or book a time that suits you, and handles everything in between: payment, the consultation, your prescription, and the follow-up. The mission is simple: **make a real consultation with a real, licensed doctor as easy as sending a message.**

## The problem it solves

Healthcare access breaks down in small, frustrating ways that add up to people not getting care when they need it:

- **Distance and time.** A routine question rarely feels worth a physical trip, so people ignore it until it worsens — or turn to unreliable self-diagnosis.
- **Trust.** It's hard to know which doctor is genuinely qualified, available, and right for your concern.
- **Waiting.** Even a nearby clinic can mean hours of waiting for minutes of consultation.
- **Continuity.** The prescription is a paper slip that gets lost, with no easy thread back for a follow-up question.
- **The other side.** Many doctors have time and expertise to offer remotely, but no simple, trusted way to consult, prescribe, and get paid for it.

HealthTok answers both sides at once. Patients get fast, trustworthy access to care from wherever they are. Doctors get a professional platform to consult, prescribe, build a reputation, and earn — without a clinic's overhead.
