## The Big Idea

Getting to see a doctor shouldn't mean a half-day lost to traffic, a crowded waiting room, and a queue that moves slower than the illness. For millions of people — especially across Nigeria and similar markets — quality healthcare is gated less by a shortage of good doctors and more by the friction of *reaching* them. HealthTok removes that friction. It puts a directory of verified doctors in your pocket, lets you talk to one in minutes (or book a time that suits you), and handles everything in between: payment, the consultation itself, your prescription, and the follow-up.

The mission is simple: **make a real consultation with a real, licensed doctor as easy as sending a message.**

## The Problem It Solves

Healthcare access is broken in small, frustrating ways that add up to people not getting care when they need it:

- **Distance and time.** A routine question — a rash, a recurring headache, a medication doubt — often isn't worth a physical trip, so people either ignore it until it worsens or turn to unreliable internet self-diagnosis.
- **Finding someone you can trust.** It's hard to know which doctor is genuinely qualified, available, and right for your concern. Word-of-mouth doesn't scale.
- **Waiting.** Even when a clinic is nearby, getting seen can mean hours of waiting for minutes of actual consultation.
- **Continuity.** After a visit, the prescription is a paper slip that gets lost, and there's no easy thread back to the doctor for a follow-up question.
- **For doctors, the other side of the same coin.** Many doctors have time and expertise they could offer remotely, but no simple, trusted way to consult patients, write prescriptions, and actually get paid for it.

HealthTok answers both sides at once. Patients get fast, trustworthy access to care from wherever they are. Doctors get a professional platform to consult, prescribe, build a reputation, and earn — without a clinic's overhead.
