## What ClearCut is

ClearCut is an image toolkit that handles the everyday chores — removing backgrounds, converting formats, resizing, and cleaning up watermarks — without uploading anything. Everything runs on your own device, so the work is automatic, free, and completely private: your images never leave your computer.

It's one tab with four tools, each built to give a clean result in seconds, with nothing to sign up for.

## What it does

- **Remove backgrounds** — drop a photo and get a clean, transparent cutout in seconds. Swap in a solid background colour if you want, then download.
- **Convert formats** — batch-convert images between the common formats, lossless or compressed.
- **Resize** — scale, crop, or compress by dimensions, a store preset, or a target file size, with a live preview of exactly what you'll get.
- **Remove watermarks** — paint over a watermark, logo, or object and it's filled in from the surrounding pixels; best on text or logos over simple areas.

## Private by design

The defining promise is that nothing is ever uploaded. Every operation happens locally, so sensitive images stay with you — no account, no server round-trip. It's automatic, free, and private.

## Who it's for

Anyone who needs quick, trustworthy image edits — creators, sellers, designers, and developers — who want fast results without handing their photos to a cloud service.
