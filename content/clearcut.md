## What ClearCut Is

ClearCut is a browser-based image toolkit that handles the everyday image chores — removing backgrounds, converting formats, resizing, and cleaning up watermarks — without uploading anything. Everything runs **on-device**, powered by in-browser AI, so the work is automatic, free, and completely private: your images never leave your computer.

It's one tab with four tools, each built to give a clean result in seconds and nothing to sign up for.

## What It Does

- **Remove backgrounds.** Drop a photo and get a clean, transparent cutout in seconds, powered by on-device AI. Swap in a solid background colour if you want, then download the PNG.
- **Convert formats.** Batch-convert any images to PNG, JPEG, WebP, or AVIF — lossless or compressed — all in the browser.
- **Resize.** Scale, crop, or compress by dimensions, a store preset, or a target file size, with a live preview of exactly what you'll get.
- **Remove watermarks.** Paint over a watermark, logo, or object and it's filled in from the surrounding pixels — on-device, and best on text or logos over simple areas.

## Private by Design

The defining promise is that nothing is ever uploaded. Every operation — background removal, conversion, resizing, watermark cleanup — happens locally in the browser, so sensitive images stay on the device. It's automatic, free, and private, with no account and no server round-trip.

## Who It's For

Anyone who needs quick, trustworthy image edits — creators, sellers, designers, and developers — who want fast results without handing their photos to a cloud service.
