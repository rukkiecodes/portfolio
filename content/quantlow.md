## What Quantlow Is

Quantlow is an AI chart-analysis tool that lives right in your browser. Point it at any trading chart — forex, crypto, stocks, or indices — and it returns a clear, structured verdict in one click: **BUY** or **SELL**, an entry, a stop, a target, the key levels around price, a confidence score and grade, and a plain-English explanation of the reasoning behind the call.

It comes in two parts that work together: a **web app**, where you upload or scan a chart, review the verdict, argue with it, and save it to your journal; and a **Chrome extension** that reads the chart on the tab you're already on — like a broker's terminal — and shows the same structured trade verdict in a side panel, including a countdown to the exact moment to enter.

## What the Scanner Gives You

Every scan produces a complete, reviewable trade idea rather than a vague signal:

- **Direction & conviction.** A BUY or SELL verdict with a confidence percentage and a letter grade, so you know how strongly the model feels about the setup.
- **The full trade.** Entry, stop loss, target, and the key levels around current price — the numbers you actually need to place and manage the trade.
- **Reasoning you can read.** A short, human explanation of *why* — trend, structure, momentum, demand and supply zones — so it's a second opinion you can judge, not a black box.
- **Invalidation.** A clear condition that voids the setup, so you know exactly when the idea is wrong and it's time to step aside.
- **Timing.** In live mode the extension counts down to the next candle, telling you when to enter, with an optional sound alert.

## Dashboard, History & Journal

Beyond one-off scans, Quantlow keeps a record. A dashboard summarises recent activity and trade feedback; every scan is saved to a history you can revisit; and you can mark how each trade actually closed — win, loss, or draw — turning the tool into a trading journal that grows alongside you. Verdicts can also be delivered straight to Telegram.

## Who It's For

Quantlow is built for active traders who want a fast, structured second opinion without leaving their chart — a disciplined, repeatable read on any setup, with the direction, entry, risk, and reasoning laid out in seconds instead of minutes.
