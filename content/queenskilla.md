## What it is

QueenSkilla is a full, production-grade two-sided marketplace connecting verified student talent with the businesses hiring them — designed and built end to end, from the product and the experience to the trust systems underneath.

## The problem

Freelance marketplaces have a trust problem in both directions. Businesses can't tell who can actually do the work, and talent — especially students with no track record — can't prove they can, or trust that they'll get paid. QueenSkilla closes both gaps at once:

- **Talent proves ability** through timed, AI-graded skill certifications and ID/face verification, then builds a portfolio automatically from real delivered work.
- **Businesses hire with confidence** — every profile is skill-tested and verified, money sits in escrow until work is approved, and any disagreement can be escalated to a reviewed dispute.

The result is a marketplace where reputation is earned and payment is guaranteed on delivery.

## What it does

**For talent (students)**

- Take timed skill assessments and earn verifiable, shareable certificates
- Get ID- and face-verified to unlock a trust badge and marketplace visibility
- Apply to projects, deliver in a real-time workspace, and get paid to a wallet
- Build a portfolio automatically — every approved project becomes a portfolio piece

**For businesses**

- Post projects with budgets and durations; browse skill-tested, verified talent
- Fund escrow up front and release only on approval, milestone by milestone
- Chat, share files, and track submissions per project
- Rate talent, and raise disputes that freeze escrow for fair resolution

**Platform-wide**

- Escrow accounting with a talent wallet and live-rate withdrawals
- In-app and push notifications with per-category controls
- A dedicated admin panel for verifications, disputes, and reports
- Verifiable certificates rendered to downloadable documents

## What makes it work

- **Escrow that actually accounts.** Funding, holding, milestone release, wallet crediting, and withdrawal are all first-class — money only moves on approval, and disputes freeze it.
- **Certification you can verify.** Skill exams are generated and graded to produce structured, checkable results, feeding a certificate system with public verification.
- **Trust as a system property.** ID and face verification, admin approval, bilateral ratings, dispute-backed escrow, and visibility gating are wired in at the core, so an untrustworthy profile simply can't surface.
- **One rule, every surface.** Whether a profile is allowed to appear is decided in exactly one place, so the same standard applies identically across web, mobile, and the leaderboard.

## In short

QueenSkilla is a marketplace, a payments system, an assessment engine, and a trust platform in one product — built to work as a real, dependable service, not a demo.
